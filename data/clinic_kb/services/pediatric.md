# Pediatric Dentistry

At Sunrise Dental Clinic, we believe that healthy smiles start early. Our pediatric dental services are designed to make every child's experience positive, comfortable, and educational — building a foundation for a lifetime of good oral health.

## When Should My Child First See a Dentist?

The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends scheduling a child's **first dental visit by age 1, or within 6 months of the first tooth erupting** — whichever comes first. Early visits help us:

- Monitor the development of teeth and jaws
- Catch problems early before they become serious
- Establish a positive relationship with dental care

## What to Expect at a Pediatric Appointment

- A gentle exam of your child's teeth, gums, jaws, and bite
- Professional cleaning appropriate for their age and tooth development
- Fluoride varnish application to strengthen enamel
- X-rays (when age-appropriate and clinically necessary)
- Education for both parent and child on brushing, flossing, and diet

## Sealants

Dental sealants are thin, protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of back molars where cavities most commonly form. We recommend sealants for children typically around **ages 6–7** (first molars) and **ages 11–13** (second molars). Sealants can reduce cavity risk by up to 80% and are quick and painless to apply.

## Fluoride Treatments

We apply fluoride varnish at each cleaning visit to help remineralize enamel and prevent decay. Additional fluoride supplements may be discussed if your child's community water supply has low fluoride content.

## Our Child-Friendly Approach

Dr. Patel specializes in making every visit fun and non-threatening. We use "tell-show-do" techniques — explaining each step before doing it, using child-friendly language, and encouraging questions. The treatment rooms feature bright colors, fun ceiling art, and entertainment options.

## Parent Presence During Treatment

Parents are welcome to accompany their child into the treatment room, especially for young children or those with dental anxiety. Dr. Patel will guide you on the best approach to help your child stay calm and cooperative.

## Insurance Coverage for Children

Most dental insurance plans provide **enhanced coverage for pediatric patients**, including 100% coverage for cleanings, fluoride, and sealants. Dr. Patel's Saturday morning pediatric clinic is specifically designed for working families.
