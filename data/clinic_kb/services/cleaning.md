# Dental Cleaning (Prophylaxis)

A professional dental cleaning — formally called a **prophylaxis** — is the cornerstone of preventive dental care. At Sunrise Dental Clinic, our skilled hygiene team provides thorough, comfortable cleanings for patients of all ages.

## What Is a Dental Cleaning?

A prophylaxis is a professional removal of plaque, tartar (calculus), and surface stains from the teeth. Unlike brushing and flossing at home, a professional cleaning reaches areas and depths that routine home care cannot address.

## How Often Should You Get a Cleaning?

The American Dental Association recommends a professional cleaning **every 6 months** for most patients. Patients with a history of gum disease, heavy buildup, or other risk factors may be recommended for more frequent visits (every 3–4 months).

## What Happens During Your Visit

1. **Medical history review** — Your hygienist reviews any health updates or medication changes
2. **Examination** — A visual check and probing of gum pockets to assess gum health
3. **Scaling** — Removal of plaque and tartar above and just below the gumline using hand scalers and/or an ultrasonic instrument
4. **Polishing** — Removal of surface stains using a gritty polishing paste and a rotating cup
5. **Flossing** — Professional flossing to clear debris between teeth
6. **Fluoride treatment** — Optional fluoride varnish applied for cavity protection
7. **X-rays** — Taken as needed (typically annually or biannually)

## Cost

- **With insurance:** Most plans cover 100% of two cleanings per year. No out-of-pocket cost for in-network patients.
- **Without insurance:** Approximately **$100–$150** for an adult cleaning. Ask about our In-House Membership Plan for uninsured patients.

## Duration

A routine adult cleaning appointment is **45–60 minutes**. Children's cleanings typically take **30–45 minutes**.

## Adult vs. Child Cleaning

Adult cleanings address heavier buildup and more complex gum assessment. Pediatric cleanings are adapted to smaller mouths, primary teeth, and the comfort needs of younger patients, performed by Dr. Patel or Dr. Singh.
