# Orthodontics & Braces

Sunrise Dental Clinic offers comprehensive orthodontic treatment for children, teens, and adults. Dr. James Lee, our board-certified orthodontist, provides personalized treatment plans to correct misalignment, crowding, spacing, and bite problems.

## Traditional Metal Braces

Metal braces remain the most effective option for complex cases and are the most affordable orthodontic treatment:

- High-grade stainless steel brackets bonded to teeth
- Arch wire applies gentle, continuous pressure to move teeth into alignment
- Monthly adjustment appointments (every 4–6 weeks)
- **Cost: approximately $3,500–$5,000** (varies by case complexity and duration)
- Most effective for severe crowding, rotations, and bite correction

## Clear/Ceramic Braces

A more discreet alternative to metal braces, using tooth-colored or clear brackets that blend with the enamel. Similar effectiveness to metal braces. Available at a slight premium.

## Invisalign Clear Aligners

See our dedicated Invisalign page for full details. In brief:

- Series of custom-made clear, removable trays
- Changed every 1–2 weeks
- **Cost: approximately $4,000–$6,000**
- Best for mild-to-moderate alignment cases

## Treatment Duration

Most orthodontic treatment at Sunrise Dental Clinic takes **12–24 months**, depending on the complexity of the case. Some minor cases can be completed in as little as 6–9 months with accelerated techniques.

## Age Recommendations

- **Children (ages 7–10):** Early evaluation recommended by the ADA at age 7 to identify problems before they worsen
- **Teens (ages 11–17):** Ideal time for treatment as permanent teeth are mostly erupted
- **Adults:** No age limit — orthodontics is effective at any age

## Retainers After Treatment

All orthodontic treatment requires retainers afterward to maintain results. We provide custom removable retainers and offer fixed (bonded) retainers for lower front teeth. Retainer wear is typically full-time for 3–6 months, then nightly indefinitely.
