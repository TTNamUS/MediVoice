# Invisalign Clear Aligners

Invisalign is the world's leading clear aligner system, offering a discreet and convenient alternative to traditional braces. At Sunrise Dental Clinic, Dr. James Lee — an **Invisalign Diamond Provider** — delivers expert Invisalign treatment to teens and adults.

## How Invisalign Works

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, clear plastic trays (aligners) that gradually shift your teeth into the desired position:

1. **Consultation & scanning:** Dr. Lee evaluates your smile and takes a 3D digital scan of your teeth (no messy impressions)
2. **Custom aligner fabrication:** Your personalized series of aligners is manufactured by Invisalign's lab
3. **Wearing your aligners:** You wear each set of trays for **1–2 weeks**, then advance to the next tray in the series
4. **Progress check-ins:** Appointments every 6–8 weeks to monitor progress and receive new aligner trays
5. **Refinements:** Minor adjustments can be made mid-treatment at no additional charge if needed

## Advantages Over Metal Braces

- **Nearly invisible:** Clear plastic is virtually undetectable — most people won't notice you're wearing them
- **Removable:** Remove aligners to eat, drink (anything other than water), brush, and floss
- **No food restrictions:** Eat whatever you like — just remove the trays first
- **More comfortable:** Smooth plastic edges — no wires to irritate cheeks or gums
- **Fewer office visits:** Check-in appointments are less frequent than braces adjustments

## Who Is a Good Candidate?

Invisalign is highly effective for mild-to-moderate cases including:

- Crowded teeth
- Spacing and gaps
- Overbite, underbite, or crossbite (moderate cases)
- Relapse after previous orthodontic treatment

Severe crowding, significant bite issues, or cases requiring major jaw correction may be better treated with traditional braces. Dr. Lee will advise you after a thorough evaluation.
