# Do You Offer Payment Plans?

**Q: I need some expensive dental work and I can't pay it all at once. Do you have payment plans?**

A: Yes — we offer several flexible options to make necessary dental treatment financially manageable.

## CareCredit Healthcare Financing

We accept **CareCredit**, a dedicated healthcare credit card that lets you pay for dental treatment over time:

- **0% interest promotional periods** — typically 6, 12, or 18 months for qualifying balances
- Apply online at carecredit.com or at our front desk in just a few minutes — approval is often instant
- Once approved, CareCredit can be used for any treatment at our office, including orthodontics, crowns, implants, and cosmetic procedures
- After the promotional period, standard interest rates apply on any remaining balance — so we encourage paying off within the promo window

## In-House Payment Plans

For treatment plans totaling **$500 or more**, we offer our own in-house payment plan:

- Payments spread over **3–6 months** depending on the total amount
- A signed payment agreement is required, along with a valid credit or debit card on file
- Plans are arranged through our billing coordinator — ask at the front desk or call (555) 743-0200

**What happens if a payment is missed?**
We will contact you by phone and email. If payment is not made within 10 days of the due date, a $25 late fee applies. If two consecutive payments are missed, the remaining balance becomes due in full. We always prefer to work with patients — please communicate with us if you are having difficulty.

## Deposit Requirements

For major procedures (implants, full-mouth treatment, orthodontics), a **25–50% deposit** of the total estimated cost is required at time of scheduling. This deposit is applied to your total balance.
