# Checking Your Insurance Coverage

**Q: How do I find out if you accept my insurance?**

A: There are a few easy ways to check:

**Option 1 — Call us directly:**
Call our billing team at **(555) 743-0200** Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Have your insurance card ready. Our team will verify within a few minutes whether we are in-network with your plan.

**Option 2 — Check our website:**
Visit sunrisedentalSJ.com/insurance for an up-to-date list of in-network plans. If your carrier is listed, we are in-network.

**Option 3 — Check with your insurer:**
Call the member services number on the back of your insurance card and ask: "Is Sunrise Dental Clinic at 1247 Sunrise Boulevard, San Jose, CA in my network?" Your insurer can give you the most accurate confirmation.

**What to bring to your appointment:**
- Your dental insurance card (front and back)
- Your insurance member ID number
- If you have secondary insurance, bring that card too

**What does "in-network" mean for my costs?**

When we are in-network with your plan, we have a contract with your insurer that sets maximum fees for each service. This typically means:

- **Lower out-of-pocket costs** for you, because we accept the contracted rate
- **Preventive services** (cleanings, X-rays, exams) are often covered at **100%** for in-network providers
- **Basic restorative** services (fillings) are often covered at **80%**
- **Major restorative** (crowns, root canals) typically covered at **50%**

Out-of-network patients can still be seen, but costs may be higher depending on your plan's out-of-network benefits.
