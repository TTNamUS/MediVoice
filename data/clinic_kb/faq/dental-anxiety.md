# Dental Anxiety & Nervous Patients

**Q: I'm really nervous about going to the dentist. How does Sunrise Dental handle anxious patients?**

A: You are not alone — dental anxiety affects millions of people, and at Sunrise Dental Clinic, we take it seriously. Our entire team is trained in techniques to help anxious patients feel safe and in control.

**Our approach to anxious patients:**

**Gentle, explain-before-you-do technique**
We always describe what we are about to do before we do it. No surprises. Knowing what to expect dramatically reduces anxiety for most patients. You are always welcome to ask us to stop and take a break — just raise your hand.

**Nitrous Oxide (Laughing Gas)**
We offer **nitrous oxide sedation** for patients who need additional help relaxing. Nitrous oxide is:
- Safe, mild, and fast-acting
- Administered through a comfortable nose mask
- Wears off within minutes — you can drive yourself home
- Available for most procedures (additional fee may apply; usually $75–$100)

Let us know when booking that you'd like nitrous oxide and we'll note it in your chart.

**Headphones and music**
You are welcome to bring your own headphones and listen to music, a podcast, or audiobook during treatment to keep your mind occupied. We also have loaner headphones available.

**Breaks on request**
You are in control. If you need a break at any point, simply raise your hand and we will stop immediately, no questions asked. There is no rush.

**Compassionate team**
Dr. Kim specializes in treating anxious patients. She takes a slow, patient approach with no judgment. Please let us know about your anxiety when booking — it helps us plan your appointment appropriately.
