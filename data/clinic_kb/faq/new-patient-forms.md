# New Patient Forms

**Q: Do I need to fill out paperwork before my first appointment?**

A: Yes — new patients are asked to complete a set of intake forms before their first visit. Completing these in advance saves time on the day of your appointment and ensures our clinical team has the information they need to provide safe, personalized care.

**Where to find the forms:**

- **Online (recommended):** Download and complete forms from our website at sunrisedentalSJ.com/new-patient — forms are available as fillable PDFs or through our online patient portal
- **In office:** If you prefer, arrive **15 minutes early** to your first appointment and fill out paper forms at the front desk

**What is included in the new patient forms:**

1. **Patient information form** — name, address, date of birth, emergency contact
2. **Medical history questionnaire** — current and past medical conditions, surgeries, medications, allergies
3. **Dental history form** — last dental visit, reason for leaving previous dentist, dental concerns
4. **Insurance authorization form** — permission to bill your insurance
5. **Financial policy acknowledgment** — signature confirming you have read our payment policies
6. **HIPAA consent form** — authorization to use your health information for treatment and billing

**Why medical history matters:**

Your medical history directly impacts your dental care. Conditions such as diabetes, heart disease, blood thinners, and bisphosphonate medications can all affect treatment decisions. Providing complete and accurate information helps us keep you safe.
