# What to Expect on Your First Visit

**Q: What should I expect at my first appointment at Sunrise Dental Clinic?**

A: Welcome! Your first visit is designed to be thorough, informative, and comfortable. Here is what to expect:

**What happens during the visit:**
1. A comprehensive oral exam by your dentist (Dr. Kim for adults, Dr. Patel for children)
2. Full-mouth X-rays (or a panoramic X-ray) to establish a baseline record of your oral health
3. A professional cleaning by Amara Singh, our lead hygienist (if same appointment is scheduled)
4. Gum health assessment including pocket depth measurements
5. Oral cancer screening
6. A personalized treatment plan discussion if any issues are found

**What to bring:**
- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
- Your insurance card (front and back)
- Completed new patient medical history form (see below)
- List of current medications and any known allergies
- Previous dental X-rays if available from your last dentist (can be emailed to us in advance)

**Arrive early:**
Please plan to arrive **15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time** to complete any remaining paperwork and get settled in.

**How long will it take?**
A comprehensive new patient exam with cleaning typically takes **60–90 minutes**. We recommend blocking 1.5 hours for your first visit.

Call (555) 743-0200 with any questions before your visit.
