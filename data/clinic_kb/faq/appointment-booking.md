# How to Book an Appointment

**Q: How do I schedule an appointment at Sunrise Dental Clinic?**

A: We offer several convenient ways to book:

**Option 1 — Phone:**
Call us at **(555) 743-0200** during business hours. Our front desk team is available Monday–Friday, 30 minutes before opening and 30 minutes after closing. We can check availability, match you with the right provider, and answer questions at the same time.

**Option 2 — Online Patient Portal:**
Book 24/7 at **sunrisedentalSJ.com/portal**. Log in (or create a new patient account) to view real-time availability and book directly. You can also manage, reschedule, or cancel existing appointments here.

**Option 3 — Voice AI Receptionist:**
Our AI receptionist is available any time of day or night to help schedule appointments, answer questions about our services, or take a message for our staff. Just call the main number after hours and follow the prompts.

**Best times to call:**
For shortest hold times, call between 9:00–10:00 AM or 2:00–3:00 PM on weekdays. Monday mornings can be busy.

**How far in advance should I book?**

- **Routine cleanings:** 1–2 weeks in advance recommended; popular times book up faster
- **New patient exams:** 1–2 weeks
- **Orthodontic consultations:** Same week typically available (free consultations with Dr. Lee)
- **Saturday pediatric slots:** 2–3 weeks in advance
- **Dental emergencies:** Call the same day — we reserve slots daily for urgent care

**Same-day emergency availability:**
If you are in pain or have a dental emergency, call us first thing in the morning at (555) 743-0200. We will do our best to see you the same day.
