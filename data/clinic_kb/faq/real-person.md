# Are You a Real Person?

**Q: Are you a real person? Am I talking to a human or a computer?**

A: That is a great question, and you deserve an honest answer.

You have reached **Sunrise Dental Clinic's AI voice receptionist**. I am an artificial intelligence assistant — not a human staff member. I am designed to help you with common tasks like scheduling appointments, answering questions about our services, policies, and hours, and taking messages for our team.

## What I Can Help You With

- Scheduling, rescheduling, or canceling appointments
- Answering questions about our services, providers, hours, and location
- Checking insurance and payment information
- Providing guidance on dental emergencies (when to call 911, what to do with a knocked-out tooth, etc.)
- Taking a message for our front desk team

## Talking to a Human Staff Member

If you would prefer to speak with a human, I completely understand. Here are your options:

**During business hours** (Monday–Friday, see our hours page for specific times):
Say "transfer to staff" or press 0 at any time to be connected to our front desk team.

**After business hours:**
Our human staff are not available, but I can take a detailed message and ensure it reaches the right person when the office opens. Your message will be returned the next business day.

**For dental emergencies after hours:**
Call our after-hours emergency line at **(555) 867-5309** to reach an on-call provider directly.

## Privacy Note

Conversations with this AI system are handled in accordance with Sunrise Dental Clinic's HIPAA-compliant privacy practices. Sensitive personal health information shared in this conversation is treated with the same confidentiality as any communication with our office.

Thank you for calling Sunrise Dental Clinic — I am here to help!
