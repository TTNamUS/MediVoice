# Am I a Good Candidate for Whitening?

**Q: Am I a good candidate for teeth whitening?**

A: Most adults with healthy teeth and gums are excellent candidates for professional whitening. Here is how to know if whitening is right for you.

## Good Candidates for Whitening

You are likely a great candidate if:

- Your teeth are generally healthy (no untreated cavities or active gum disease)
- Your staining is primarily **extrinsic** (surface stains from coffee, tea, wine, tobacco)
- Your enamel is intact (not significantly worn or thinned)
- You do not have dental restorations on your front teeth (crowns, veneers, bonding — see below)
- You are not pregnant or breastfeeding (whitening is not recommended during pregnancy)

## Who Is NOT a Good Candidate?

Whitening may not be suitable if:

- **You have crowns, veneers, or bonding on your visible front teeth.** Whitening gel only affects natural tooth enamel — restorations will not change color and will appear darker than your newly whitened teeth, creating a noticeable mismatch
- **You have intrinsic (deep) staining** such as tetracycline antibiotic staining (grayish banding) or fluorosis (white/brown spots). These stains are inside the tooth and do not respond well to peroxide-based whitening
- **You have untreated cavities or gum disease.** These must be treated first; whitening gel can penetrate damaged enamel and cause significant pain
- **You are under age 16.** Tooth roots are not fully developed and whitening is not recommended

## Alternatives for Non-Candidates

If whitening is not suitable for you, we may recommend:

- **Dental bonding** — tooth-colored resin applied to stained or chipped teeth
- **Porcelain veneers** — thin shells that cover the front surface of teeth for a complete smile transformation
- **Microabrasion** — for superficial fluorosis spots

## Managing Sensitivity

Sensitivity during and after whitening is common but manageable. We recommend starting desensitizing toothpaste (Sensodyne) one week before treatment. We also include desensitizing gel with our professional kits.
