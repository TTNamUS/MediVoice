# Dr. Michael Nguyen, DDS — Emergency & Endodontics

Dr. Michael Nguyen is Sunrise Dental Clinic's emergency care and endodontics specialist, bringing **16 years of experience** in high-urgency dental situations. His calm demeanor under pressure and expert pain management skills make him the provider patients trust when dental emergencies strike.

## Education & Training

- **Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS)** — UCSF School of Dentistry, 2009
- Advanced training in endodontics (root canal therapy) and endodontic microsurgery
- Certified in Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS)
- Advanced training in dental trauma management
- Member, American Association of Endodontists (AAE)
- Member, California Dental Association (CDA)

## Areas of Focus

- Emergency dental care (same-day and walk-in)
- Root canal treatment (anterior and posterior teeth, including molars)
- Endodontic retreatment (retreating previously treated canals)
- Dental abscess management and incision & drainage
- Cracked tooth diagnosis and treatment
- Dental trauma (knocked-out, luxated, and fractured teeth)

## Languages

Dr. Nguyen sees patients in **English and Vietnamese**.

## Emergency Walk-Ins

Dr. Nguyen accommodates **same-day emergency walk-in appointments**. Patients in acute pain or with dental trauma are triaged immediately upon arrival. Call ahead when possible so we can prepare, but walk-ins in distress are always welcome.

## About Dr. Nguyen

Dr. Nguyen was drawn to emergency and endodontic care because he loves helping patients out of pain quickly. He is known for his exceptionally gentle injection technique and his ability to keep anxious emergency patients calm. Outside dentistry, he is an avid cyclist and volunteers at community health fairs offering free dental screenings.
