# Dr. Sarah Kim, DDS — General Dentistry

Dr. Sarah Kim is Sunrise Dental Clinic's lead general dentist, bringing over **15 years of experience** in comprehensive dental care. She is widely recognized for her calm, reassuring chairside manner and her dedication to making every patient feel at ease.

## Education & Training

- **Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS)** — UCLA School of Dentistry, 2010
- Continuing education focus: restorative dentistry, cosmetic techniques, and dental anxiety management
- Member, California Dental Association (CDA) and American Dental Association (ADA)

## Areas of Focus

- Preventive care and routine exams
- Composite fillings and tooth-colored restorations
- Crowns, bridges, and fixed restorations
- Dental anxiety management and gentle care
- Patient education and oral health coaching

## Languages

Dr. Kim sees patients in **English and Korean**.

## About Dr. Kim

Dr. Kim grew up in the South Bay area and returned after dental school to serve the San Jose community she loves. She is especially passionate about treating anxious patients, using a slow, explain-before-you-do approach that helps fearful patients regain confidence in dental care. Outside the office, she is an avid hiker and volunteers with community dental outreach programs.
