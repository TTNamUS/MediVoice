# Amara Singh, RDH — Lead Dental Hygienist

Amara Singh is the lead dental hygienist at Sunrise Dental Clinic. While patients often affectionately refer to her as "Dr. Singh," Amara holds a **Registered Dental Hygienist (RDH)** credential — a highly trained clinical professional specializing in preventive oral care and periodontal health.

## Education & Training

- **Bachelor of Science in Dental Hygiene** — UCSF Dental Hygiene Program, 2012
- Certified in Local Anesthesia Administration (California RDH permit)
- Advanced training in periodontal assessment and scaling & root planing
- Certified in Laser-Assisted Periodontal Therapy
- Member, American Dental Hygienists' Association (ADHA)

## Areas of Focus

- Adult and adolescent prophylaxis (routine cleanings)
- Periodontal disease assessment and management
- Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning for gum disease)
- Periodontal maintenance therapy (every 3–4 months for gum disease patients)
- Patient education on oral hygiene techniques, diet, and home care
- Fluoride and sealant application
- Oral cancer screening

## About Amara Singh

Amara has been with Sunrise Dental Clinic since its founding and is the heartbeat of the hygiene department. She is renowned for her gentle technique and her remarkable ability to make even the most tartar-encrusted cleaning feel manageable and comfortable. She is deeply committed to periodontal disease prevention and spends significant time educating each patient on the systemic connections between gum health and overall wellbeing — including heart disease and diabetes.
