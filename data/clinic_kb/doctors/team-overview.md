# Our Dental Team at Sunrise Dental Clinic

Sunrise Dental Clinic is home to a team of experienced, caring dentists and specialists. Here is an overview of all our providers.

## Dr. Sarah Kim, DDS — General Dentistry

Dr. Sarah Kim is our lead general dentist with over 15 years of experience. She sees patients on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, and is currently accepting new patients. She speaks English and Korean.

## Dr. Priya Patel, DMD — Pediatric Dentistry

Dr. Priya Patel is our board-certified pediatric dentist. She specializes in children's dental care from infancy through adolescence. She sees patients Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday mornings. She speaks English, Gujarati, and Hindi.

## Dr. James Lee, DDS, MS — Orthodontics

Dr. James Lee is our orthodontist and an Invisalign Diamond Provider. He offers metal braces, ceramic braces, and Invisalign clear aligners. He sees patients Monday and Thursday. He speaks English and Mandarin.
