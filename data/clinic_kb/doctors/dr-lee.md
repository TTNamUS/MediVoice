# Dr. James Lee, DMD, MS — Orthodontics & Invisalign

Dr. James Lee is Sunrise Dental Clinic's orthodontist and a recognized leader in clear aligner therapy in the South Bay. As an **Invisalign Diamond Provider**, Dr. Lee is among the top-tier of certified Invisalign practitioners nationwide, reflecting the high volume and quality of his clear aligner cases.

## Education & Training

- **Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD)** — Tufts University School of Dental Medicine, 2011
- **Master of Science (MS) in Orthodontics** — Orthodontic Residency, University of Southern California (USC), 2013
- Invisalign Diamond Provider (top 1% of providers by case volume)
- Member, American Association of Orthodontists (AAO)
- Member, Pacific Coast Society of Orthodontists

## Areas of Focus

- Traditional metal and ceramic braces (children, teens, adults)
- Invisalign clear aligners for teens and adults
- Early interceptive orthodontics (Phase I, ages 7–10)
- Retainer fabrication and post-treatment monitoring
- Complex bite correction and jaw alignment

## Languages

Dr. Lee is known for his meticulous attention to detail and his ability to explain complex treatment plans in clear, relatable terms. He treats patients from age 7 through adulthood and loves seeing the confidence that comes with a straighter smile.
