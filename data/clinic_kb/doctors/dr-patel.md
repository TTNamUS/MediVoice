# Dr. Priya Patel, DDS, MS — Pediatric Dentistry

Dr. Priya Patel is Sunrise Dental Clinic's board-certified pediatric dental specialist. Her passion for working with children, combined with advanced clinical training, makes her the go-to provider for families throughout the San Jose area.

## Education & Training

- **Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS)** — NYU College of Dentistry, 2013
- **Master of Science (MS) in Pediatric Dentistry** — Pediatric Residency, Boston Children's Hospital / Harvard School of Dental Medicine, 2015
- Board Certified, American Board of Pediatric Dentistry (ABPD)
- Special training in treating children with special needs and complex medical histories
- Advanced training in pediatric dental anxiety management and behavioral guidance

## Areas of Focus

- Infant and toddler dental exams (starting at age 1)
- Preventive care: cleanings, fluoride, sealants
- Pediatric restorations (fillings, stainless steel crowns)
- Early orthodontic evaluation
- Special needs dentistry
- Dental anxiety and behavior management (without sedation when possible)

## Languages

Dr. Patel sees patients in **English, Gujarati, and Hindi**.

## About Dr. Patel

Dr. Patel is known throughout the clinic for her warm, playful energy that puts children instantly at ease. She believes that a positive early dental experience sets the tone for a lifetime of good oral health habits. She has a special place in her heart for treating children with disabilities and anxiety disorders, and has completed extensive additional training to serve these families with patience and skill.
