# Cancellation Policy

At Sunrise Dental Clinic, we value your time and the time of our providers and other patients. To ensure we can accommodate everyone's scheduling needs, we maintain the following cancellation policy.

## 24-Hour Cancellation Requirement

All appointment cancellations must be made at least **24 hours before your scheduled appointment time**. This allows us to offer your appointment slot to another patient who may be waiting for care.

## Cancellation Fees

- **Late Cancellation (less than 24 hours notice):** $50 fee
- **No-Show (no contact made):** $75 fee — see our separate No-Show Policy for details

These fees are charged to the card on file or invoiced directly and are not covered by dental insurance.

## How to Cancel

You may cancel your appointment through any of the following methods:

1. **Phone:** Call us at (555) 743-0200 during business hours. Leave a voicemail if calling after hours — as long as the message is received 24 hours before your appointment, no fee applies.
2. **Online Patient Portal:** Log in at sunrisedentalSJ.com/portal and cancel directly through the scheduling section.
3. **Voice AI Receptionist:** Our 24/7 AI receptionist can process cancellations at any hour.

## Exceptions for Emergencies

We understand that genuine emergencies happen. Medical emergencies, hospitalizations, and other documented urgent situations may be eligible for a fee waiver. Contact our office as soon as possible and speak with our front desk manager to discuss your circumstances.

## Rescheduling

We encourage rescheduling rather than canceling when possible. You can reschedule through the same channels listed above. We recommend rescheduling your cleaning or checkup within 4–6 weeks to stay on track with your oral health plan.
