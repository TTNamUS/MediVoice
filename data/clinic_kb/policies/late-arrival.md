# Late Arrival Policy

We understand that unexpected delays happen. Sunrise Dental Clinic makes every effort to accommodate patients who arrive late, while being fair to providers and other patients with scheduled appointments.

## Grace Period

We allow a **10-minute grace period** for late arrivals. If you arrive within 10 minutes of your scheduled appointment time, we will do our best to see you with minimal disruption.

## Arrivals More Than 10 Minutes Late

If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, your appointment may need to be **rescheduled**. This decision is made on a case-by-case basis depending on:

- The nature and length of your scheduled procedure
- Provider availability for the remainder of the day
- Impact on patients with appointments following yours

In some cases, we may be able to complete a shorter portion of your planned treatment (e.g., exam without cleaning) and reschedule the remaining procedures.

## How Late Arrivals Affect Your Appointment

Running late may reduce the time available for your procedure. For example, a routine cleaning scheduled for 60 minutes may be shortened to 30–40 minutes if you arrive late, which could mean certain steps are deferred to a follow-up visit. This is not ideal for your oral health, so prompt arrival is strongly encouraged.

## How to Notify Us

If you know you will be late, please call us as soon as possible at **(555) 743-0200** so we can do our best to plan accordingly. Notifying us even 5 minutes in advance helps our team manage the schedule more effectively.

We recommend arriving **10–15 minutes early** for all appointments, especially first visits.
