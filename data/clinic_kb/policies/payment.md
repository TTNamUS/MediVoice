# Payment Options

Sunrise Dental Clinic is committed to making dental care financially accessible. We offer a variety of payment methods and financing options to fit your needs.

## Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following forms of payment:

- **Major Credit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Debit Cards**
- **Cash**
- **Personal Check** (made payable to Sunrise Dental Clinic)
- **HSA / FSA Cards** (Health Savings Account and Flexible Spending Account cards are accepted and recommended for eligible dental expenses)

Payment is collected at time of service for your estimated patient responsibility after insurance.

## CareCredit Financing

We proudly accept **CareCredit**, a healthcare credit card that allows you to pay for dental treatments over time with convenient monthly payments. CareCredit frequently offers **0% interest promotional periods** (typically 6, 12, or 18 months) for qualifying balances. Apply at carecredit.com or ask our front desk staff to assist you with the application in office — approval takes only a few minutes.

## In-House Payment Plans

For larger treatment plans totaling **$500 or more**, we offer an in-house payment plan option. Plans are arranged through our billing coordinator and typically spread payments over 3–6 months. A signed payment agreement and a valid card on file are required.

## Deposits for Major Procedures

Major restorative or cosmetic procedures (such as implants, full crowns, or orthodontic treatment) require a **deposit of 25–50%** of the total treatment cost at the time of scheduling. This deposit is applied toward your total balance.

## Receipts

Itemized receipts are provided after every payment and are also available through the patient portal at sunrisedentalSJ.com/portal. These receipts are suitable for HSA/FSA reimbursement submissions.
