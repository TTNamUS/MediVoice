# No-Show Policy

A "no-show" is defined as failing to appear for a scheduled appointment without contacting our office beforehand. No-shows prevent other patients from being seen and affect the quality of care we can provide to our community.

## No-Show Fee

A **$75 no-show fee** is charged for each missed appointment without prior notice. This fee is not billable to insurance and is the patient's sole responsibility.

## First-Time Waiver

We understand that emergencies and genuine oversights happen. Your **first no-show fee may be waived** if you contact our office within 24 hours of the missed appointment and provide a reasonable explanation. Please call (555) 743-0200 to speak with our front desk team. This courtesy waiver is available **once per patient**.

## Repeated No-Shows

We take a progressive approach to repeated no-shows:

- **2nd no-show:** Fee charged, written notice sent to patient
- **3rd no-show:** Fee charged, and the patient may be **dismissed from the practice**

Patients who are dismissed due to repeated no-shows will be provided with a 30-day window to establish care with a new dental provider and may request a copy of their records at no charge during this period.

## How to Appeal

If you believe a no-show fee was applied in error or you have extenuating circumstances (such as a medical emergency or hospitalization), you may submit a written appeal to our office manager at manager@sunrisedentalSJ.com or in writing to our office. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.

We encourage patients to cancel in advance whenever possible — even a same-day call is better than a no-show and helps us offer your slot to a patient in need.
