# Insurance & Billing

Sunrise Dental Clinic works with a wide range of dental insurance providers to make quality dental care accessible and affordable for our patients.

## Accepted Insurance Plans

We are proud to be in-network with the following major dental insurance carriers:

- **Delta Dental** (PPO and Premier plans)
- **Cigna Dental**
- **Aetna Dental**
- **MetLife Dental**
- **Guardian**
- **United Concordia**

We also accept many other PPO plans as out-of-network providers. Please call our billing team at (555) 743-0200 to verify your specific plan.

## Insurance Verification Process

When you schedule a new appointment, our billing team will request your insurance information in advance. We submit a pre-verification request to confirm your coverage, annual maximums, deductibles, and co-pay amounts before your visit. You will be informed of your estimated patient responsibility prior to treatment.

Please bring your insurance card and a valid photo ID to every appointment.

## In-Network vs. Out-of-Network

**In-network patients** benefit from pre-negotiated rates, meaning your out-of-pocket costs are typically lower. Your insurance pays the contracted rate and you pay the remainder up to your plan limits.

**Out-of-network patients** may still receive care at Sunrise Dental. We will submit claims on your behalf, and your insurer will reimburse you or us based on their out-of-network reimbursement rate. Patients are responsible for any balance not covered.

## Coordination of Benefits (COB)

If you carry two insurance plans (e.g., through your employer and a spouse's employer), we will coordinate benefits between both carriers. Please provide both insurance cards at check-in. Our billing team will handle primary and secondary claim submission.

## Patient Responsibility & Billing Cycle

After your insurance claim is processed — typically within 30 days — any remaining balance will be billed to you by mail and email. Statements are sent on the 1st and 15th of each month. Payment is due within 30 days of statement date. For questions about your bill, contact our billing department Monday–Friday 9 AM–5 PM.
