# Parking Information

Sunrise Dental Clinic is committed to making your visit as convenient as possible, and that starts with easy, affordable parking.

## Building Garage — Free Validated Parking

**Free parking is available in the Sunrise Medical Arts Building garage** for all patients.

- **Garage entrance:** Enter from Sunrise Boulevard — look for the "Parking" sign on the north side of the building
- **Validation:** Bring your parking ticket inside to the front desk, and we will validate it for up to **2 hours** of free parking
- **Levels:** The garage has 4 levels; patient spots are available on all levels. We recommend Level 1 or Level 2 for easiest elevator access
- **Hours:** Garage opens at 7:00 AM and closes at 8:00 PM on weekdays; 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM on Saturdays

For appointments expected to exceed 2 hours (e.g., multiple procedures in one visit), the garage rate is **$2.00/hour** after validation expires.

## Street Parking

Metered street parking is available on **Oak Street** (running parallel to Sunrise Blvd, one block east):

- Rate: **$1.50 per hour**
- Maximum stay: 2 hours
- Meters operate Monday–Saturday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
- Free on Sundays (clinic is closed Sundays)

## Accessible Parking

**Handicap-designated spaces** are located on the **ground floor of the building garage**, directly adjacent to the elevator lobby. Standard ADA signage marks these spaces. A valid disabled placard or license plate is required.

## Motorcycle Parking

Designated motorcycle parking is available near the elevator on the ground floor of the garage, at no charge.
