# Accessibility & ADA Compliance

Sunrise Dental Clinic and the Sunrise Medical Arts Building are fully compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). We are committed to providing a welcoming, barrier-free environment for all patients, including those with mobility, sensory, and cognitive disabilities.

## Building Access

- **Wheelchair-accessible entrance** on the ground floor of the building, accessible directly from the parking garage and from Sunrise Boulevard via a ramped curb cut
- **Automatic door openers** at the main building entrance and our suite entrance on the third floor
- **Elevator** serving all floors — located immediately adjacent to the accessible entrance; buttons include Braille labels
- Elevator dimensions accommodate standard and power wheelchairs

## Suite 300 — Our Office

- Wide doorways throughout the clinic to accommodate wheelchairs and mobility aids
- **Accessible reception desk** with a lowered section for wheelchair users
- **Accessible restrooms** on the third floor with grab bars, wide stalls, and lowered fixtures
- Treatment rooms are designed with **extra clearance** around the dental chair for wheelchair transfers
- **Adjustable dental chairs** that can be positioned at a low height to facilitate patient transfers
- Non-slip flooring throughout the office

## Communication & Sensory Support

- **Sign language interpretation** is available for Deaf and hard-of-hearing patients. Please request this service at least **48 hours in advance** when booking your appointment so we can arrange a certified interpreter.
- **Large-print forms** are available at the front desk upon request for patients with low vision
- Staff are trained to communicate clearly and patiently with patients who have cognitive disabilities or communication differences
- Quiet waiting area accommodations available for patients with sensory sensitivities — just let us know at scheduling

## Special Needs Patients

Dr. Priya Patel has advanced training in treating pediatric patients with special needs. For adult patients with complex disability-related needs, please speak with our office manager before your appointment so we can plan appropriately.
