# Public Transit Directions

Sunrise Dental Clinic is accessible via multiple public transit options in the San Jose area. We encourage patients to use public transportation when convenient — it's good for the environment and avoids parking hassles.

## VTA Bus — Closest Stop

**VTA Bus Route 23 (Stevens Creek Boulevard)**

This is the most convenient transit option. Route 23 runs along Stevens Creek Boulevard, with a stop approximately **2 blocks from our office**:

- **Stop:** Stevens Creek Blvd & Sunrise Blvd (northbound/southbound)
- **Walk from stop:** Approximately 3–4 minutes on foot
- Route 23 runs frequently during weekday hours; check 511.org for real-time departures

**VTA Bus Route 60 (Winchester Boulevard)**

Route 60 stops on Winchester Boulevard, approximately **10 minutes walking distance** east of our office:

- **Stop:** Winchester Blvd & Moorpark Ave
- **Walk from stop:** Head west on Stevens Creek Blvd, then north on Sunrise Blvd — approximately 0.5 miles

## VTA Light Rail

**Winchester Station — VTA Blue Line**

The nearest light rail station is **Winchester Station** on the VTA Blue Line:

- Distance from clinic: approximately **0.8 miles**
- Walking time: approximately **15–18 minutes**
- Alternative: Take a short rideshare (Uber/Lyft) from Winchester Station to our office — typically 3–5 minutes and under $7

## BART Connections

BART does not directly serve the Westfield Valley Fair area. The closest BART-to-VTA transfer point is:

- **Milpitas BART Station** → Transfer to VTA Bus Route 22 (Stevens Creek) westbound → Approximately 25 minutes to our area

Plan your full transit journey at **511.org** or the Google Maps transit option.
