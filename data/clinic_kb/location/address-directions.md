# Address & Directions

## Our Address

**Sunrise Dental Clinic**
1247 Sunrise Boulevard, Suite 300
San Jose, CA 95128

**Phone:** (555) 743-0200
**Website:** sunrisedentalSJ.com

## Landmark

We are located in the **Sunrise Medical Arts Building**, directly **across from Westfield Valley Fair Mall** — one of the largest shopping centers in the South Bay. If you can see the mall, look for the 3-story beige professional building on the opposite side of Sunrise Boulevard. We are on the **third floor, Suite 300**.

## Driving Directions

**From Highway 880 (eastbound or westbound):**
1. Exit at **Stevens Creek Boulevard** and head east
2. Continue on Stevens Creek Blvd for approximately **0.5 miles**
3. Turn right onto **Sunrise Boulevard**
4. The Sunrise Medical Arts Building will be on your right in approximately 200 feet
5. Enter the building garage from Sunrise Blvd

**From US-101:**
1. Take the **Winchester Boulevard** exit and head north
2. Continue on Winchester Blvd for approximately 1.2 miles
3. Turn left onto **Sunrise Boulevard**
4. Continue 0.3 miles — the building will be on your left

**From I-280:**
1. Exit at **Winchester Boulevard** and head north
2. Follow the same directions as from US-101 above
