# Holiday Closures

Sunrise Dental Clinic observes all major United States federal holidays. We close our offices on these days to give our staff time with their families and to observe national holidays alongside our community.

## Annual Holiday Closure Schedule

| Holiday                   | Date (approximate)         |
|---------------------------|----------------------------|
| New Year's Day            | January 1                  |
| Martin Luther King Jr. Day | Third Monday in January    |
| Presidents' Day           | Third Monday in February   |
| Memorial Day              | Last Monday in May         |
| Independence Day          | July 4                     |
| Labor Day                 | First Monday in September  |
| Thanksgiving Day          | Fourth Thursday in November|
| Day After Thanksgiving    | Fourth Friday in November  |
| Christmas Eve             | December 24                |
| Christmas Day             | December 25                |

*When a federal holiday falls on a weekend, we close the nearest weekday (Friday or Monday).*

## Patient Reminders Near Holidays

If you have an appointment scheduled within the week before or after a major holiday, our front desk team will call you **48 hours in advance** to confirm your appointment and remind you of any adjusted schedule or closure. You will also receive an automated email and/or text reminder.

## Rescheduling Around Holidays

We understand holidays can create scheduling challenges. If you need to reschedule an appointment that conflicts with holiday plans, please contact us as early as possible. Holiday weeks tend to fill quickly as many patients want appointments before or after closures. We recommend scheduling 3–4 weeks in advance during November and December.

## Emergency Care During Holidays

Our after-hours emergency line **(555) 867-5309** remains available on most holidays for urgent dental situations.
