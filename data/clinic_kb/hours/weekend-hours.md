# Weekend & Saturday Hours

## Saturday Hours

Sunrise Dental Clinic is open on **Saturday mornings from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM** for pediatric appointments only.

Saturday hours are exclusively staffed by **Dr. Priya Patel**, our board-certified pediatric dentist. These slots are specifically designed for families who cannot bring children to weekday appointments due to school and work schedules.

**Services available on Saturdays:**
- Pediatric dental exams and cleanings
- Fluoride treatments
- Sealant applications
- Pediatric X-rays (when indicated)
- Non-urgent pediatric restorations (fillings)

Adult patients and non-pediatric services are **not available on Saturdays**. Adult patients must schedule appointments Monday through Friday.

## Book Saturday Appointments Early

Saturday slots are extremely popular and limited. We strongly recommend booking **2–3 weeks in advance** to secure a Saturday appointment. Call (555) 743-0200 Monday–Friday during business hours to schedule, or book online via sunrisedentalSJ.com/portal.

## Sunday Hours

We are **closed on Sundays**. No in-office appointments or phone services are available on Sundays.

## Weekend Emergencies

For dental emergencies on weekends, call our after-hours emergency line at **(555) 867-5309**. This line is monitored on weekends for urgent situations. For life-threatening emergencies (difficulty breathing, severe facial trauma), call 911 or go to the nearest hospital emergency room.
