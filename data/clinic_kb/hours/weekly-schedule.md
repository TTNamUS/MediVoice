# Weekly Office Hours

Sunrise Dental Clinic maintains a schedule designed to serve our diverse patient community, with extended weekday hours and Saturday morning pediatric availability.

## Regular Weekly Schedule

| Day       | Hours              | Providers Available                      |
|-----------|--------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Monday    | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM  | Dr. Kim, Dr. Lee, Amara Singh (Hygiene)  |
| Tuesday   | 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM  | Dr. Patel, Dr. Nguyen, Amara Singh       |
| Wednesday | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM  | Dr. Kim, Dr. Nguyen, Amara Singh         |
| Thursday  | 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM  | Dr. Lee, Amara Singh                     |
| Friday    | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM  | Dr. Kim, Dr. Nguyen                      |
| Saturday  | 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM  | Dr. Patel only (pediatric appointments)  |
| Sunday    | Closed             | —                                        |

## Phone Hours

Our front desk phone lines open **30 minutes before the clinic opens** and remain available **30 minutes after the clinic closes** each weekday. For example, on Monday phones are available from 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM.

On Saturdays, phones open at 8:30 AM and close at 1:30 PM.

## Scheduling Tips

- **Early morning appointments** (8:00–9:00 AM) are popular — book 1–2 weeks in advance for Mon/Wed/Fri
- **Tuesday evening slots** (after 5:00 PM) with Dr. Nguyen are ideal for working patients
- **Saturday pediatric slots** with Dr. Patel are extremely limited — book 2–3 weeks in advance
- For routine cleanings with Amara Singh, availability Mon–Thu is generally good with 1 week lead time

## After-Hours Emergencies

For dental emergencies outside of office hours, call our after-hours line: **(555) 867-5309**. This line is monitored evenings and weekends for urgent dental situations.
