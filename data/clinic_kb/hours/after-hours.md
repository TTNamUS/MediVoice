# After-Hours & Emergency Contact

Dental emergencies don't follow business hours. Sunrise Dental Clinic maintains an after-hours emergency line to ensure our patients can reach a dental professional when urgent situations arise outside of regular office hours.

## After-Hours Emergency Line

**Phone: (555) 867-5309**

This line is monitored during the following times:

- **Monday – Friday:** 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM (after office closes)
- **Saturday:** 1:00 PM – 8:00 PM (after Saturday clinic closes)
- **Sunday:** 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

When you call, you will reach an answering service that connects your message to the on-call provider. Dr. Michael Nguyen typically manages after-hours emergency calls. Response time is generally **within 30–60 minutes**.

## What Qualifies for an After-Hours Call?

Please use the after-hours line for **true dental emergencies**, including:

- Severe toothache or dental pain preventing sleep
- Knocked-out or displaced tooth (time-sensitive — call immediately)
- Swelling of the face, jaw, or neck (may indicate spreading infection)
- Broken tooth with a sharp edge causing injury to soft tissue
- Lost or broken dental appliance causing significant discomfort or risk

## Non-Emergency Calls

For non-emergency matters (appointment scheduling, billing questions, prescription refill requests), please call our main line **(555) 743-0200** during regular business hours. Non-emergency after-hours messages will be returned **the next business day**.

## When to Call 911 or Go to the ER

Contact emergency services or go to the hospital emergency room immediately for:

- Difficulty breathing or swallowing (possible airway obstruction from swelling)
- Uncontrolled bleeding that does not stop with pressure
- Suspected broken jaw or significant facial fracture
- High fever with facial swelling (sign of serious spreading infection)
- Any dental injury occurring alongside a head or neck injury

Hospital emergency rooms handle life-threatening situations. After stabilization, contact our office for follow-up dental care.
